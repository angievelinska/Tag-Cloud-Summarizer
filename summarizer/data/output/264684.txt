

The CoreMedia RichText component provides:

extended text formatting
3* superscript, subscript, strike-through in addition to bold, italic and normal formatting
3* the assignment of styles such as font, font color and background color
WYSIWIG list editing
3* bulleted and numbered lists, also nested lists, blockquotes
WYSIWYG table editing
Convenient ways to insert and locate images.
Spellchecking
Internal and external links
