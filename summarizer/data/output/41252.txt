

Select the document with a click in on it the overview window. To open a selected document:

Select File|Open from the menu bar
Select Open from the context menu
Press the key combination <Ctrl>+<o>.
Double-click the document in the overview window. In this way you can also open a document which was not selected before.

The document opens in a document window. The status and name of the document can be seen in it's title bar.
