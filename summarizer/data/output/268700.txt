

Correct spelling errors

In order to correct misspelled words, you have to check-out the document and start the spellchecking. Proceed as follows:
Left-click an underlined word. A combo box opens up and shows suggestions (if there are any).
Click a suggestions or correct the word manually. Press the Enter-key to take the correction.
If you do not want to correct the word, press the Esc-key.
