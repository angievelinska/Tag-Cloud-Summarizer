

To replace one term with another, 


Check out the document.


Choose Edit | Replace, or



<CTRL>+<F8> or <ALT>+<e>.


3#


Type in the search term, the replace term, and check the options (see illustration). 


Click [Search] to begin.


After a term was found, you may



click [Replace] to replace the term


click [Search] to continue the search without replacing this one term, or


click [Cancel] to cancel the search and replace procedure.


3* If all terms have been replaced, this is indicated. 


6#


Finish or cancel the search by clicking [Cancel].
