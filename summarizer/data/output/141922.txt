

You can use stylesheet attributes to assign an attribute to your text. This attribute will be evaluated during output and will affect the appearance of the output. To format a text section or a word with a style sheet attribute, proceed as follows:


Highlight the text section which you want to format.


Open the dialog for stylesheet attribute assignment in one of the following ways:





Select Format|Stylesheet attribute from the menu bar or Stylesheet attribute from the Context menu.


Click  and  on the toolbar.


Use the keyboard shortcut <Ctrl>+<Shift>+<s>.

A dialog box appears for the stylesheet attribute.
