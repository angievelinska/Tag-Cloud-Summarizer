

To search for a term,


Check out the document


Choose Edit|Search, or



press <CTRL>+<F5> or <ALT>+<S>.


3#


Type in the search term and check the options (see illustrationc). 



3* After a term was found, the search has to be continued by clicking [Search].


4#


Finish or cancel the search by clicking [Cancel].



3* If no matching term was found, an error message says so.
