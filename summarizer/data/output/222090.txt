

The CoreMedia Smart Content Technology has been developed to provide a convincing and universal solution for the creation and management of content.

The use of powerful development tools and open interfaces enables the system to be adapted flexibly to enterprise requirements. For this purpose, world-wide standards for information transfer, such as XML, HTML, HTTP, WebDAV, CORBA and the Java Platform are used or supported.
