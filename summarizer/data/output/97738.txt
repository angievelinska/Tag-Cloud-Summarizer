


 

Menu item
 

Sub items
 

Functions
 

Cut
 


 

Copies the selected resource(s) from the change list to the clipboard. 
 

Copy
 


 

Copies the selected resource to the clipboard. A copy will be pasted to the target position.
 

Paste
 


 

Pastes the content of the clipboard to the current position of the change list. It is not possible to paste documents in documents but in folders.
