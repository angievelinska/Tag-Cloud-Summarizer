

The Document window consists of the following components:

Menu bar (1) 
You can reach all functionality concerning the WebEditor and the document from the menu bar. You have no access to the formatting functions from the menu bar
Toolbar (2) 
You have fast access to document specific functionality
Document name (3)
Second Toolbar (4)
You may save, check-in, check-out or revoke check-out of the document.
Third Toolbar (5)
You have access to the document field specific functionality.
The document fields (6)
You can enter the content to the document.
