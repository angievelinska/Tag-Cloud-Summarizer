Insert images from the CoreMedia repositiory

In RichText documents you can insert images - CoreMedia Picture documents - directly in text field at the current cursor position:
Open the document in the document window
Move the cursor to the position
Insert the image either

by choosing Format | Insert image at current position, or
clicking  and  Insert an image... in the tool bar.
