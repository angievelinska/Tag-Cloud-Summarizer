

 Protocol Entry jmxmp 

service:jmx:jmxmp://localhost:<Port>

Replace <Port> with a free port. Hessian 

service:jmx:hessian://<ContentServerHost>:<ContentServerPort>/coremedia/jmx-hessian

or for a secure SSL connection

service:jmx:hessian+ssl://<ContentServerHost>:<ContentServerSSLPort>/coremedia/jmx-hessian-ssl

Replace <ContentServerHost> and <ContentServerPort> or <ContentServerSSLPort> with the settings of the properties cap.server.http.host and cap.server.http.port or cap.server.https.port in the contentserver.properties file.
