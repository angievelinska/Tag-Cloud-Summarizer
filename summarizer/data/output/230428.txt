

The entryReferer_pageflow  bean takes care of saving the Referer header of the request in the pageflow.

With these steps you have finished the setup of the example. Now you may call the URL of your application, for example: http://localhost:8001/myWebapp/views/first.jsp. The first view might be a log in dialog where you need to log in for you editing services environment. On the next page you will see the link pointing to a page with a simple button, which redirects back to the entry page.
