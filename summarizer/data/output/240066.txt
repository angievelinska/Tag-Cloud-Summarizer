

 Protocol Entry jmxmp 

service:jmx:jmxmp://localhost:<Port>

Replace <Port> with a free port. Hessian 

service:jmx:hessian://<WorkflowServerHost>:<WorkflowServerPort>/workflow/jmx-hessian

or for a secure SSL connection

service:jmx:hessian+ssl://<WorkflowServerHost>:<WorkflowServerSSLPort>/workflow/jmx-hessian-ssl

Replace <WorkflowServerHost> and <WorkflowServerPort> or <WorkflowServerSSLPort> with the settings of the properties workflow.server.http.host and workflow.server.http.port or workflow.server.https.port in the workflowserver.properties file.
